About NAMSACCO

NAMSACCO was registered on 10 April 2018 as a Savings and Credit Co-operative under the Co-operatives Act of 1996. Membership is voluntary and open to public servants from government offices, ministries, agencies and regional councils.

Membership Benefits

  • Affordable loan facilities.
  • Dividends on shares and savings.
  • Insurance on outstanding loans.
  • Financial education and training.
  • Participation in general meetings.
  • Opportunity to elect leaders.
  • Become a shareholder in your organisation.

Loan Products

Development Loans

  • Up to 3 times savings.
  • 15% per annum.
  • Maximum repayment period of 60 months.
  • Land, livestock, buildings and vehicles.

School Fees Loans

  • Schools, colleges and universities.
  • 12 months repayment period.
  • Supporting documents required.

Emergency Loans

  • Medical expenses.
  • Funeral expenses.
  • Fire disasters.
  • Approved within two working days.
  • Maximum amount N$20,000.

Shares & Savings

Shares

Minimum share capital consists of 100 shares valued at N$20 each.

Savings

Minimum monthly savings contribution is N$200. Members may save above the minimum.

  • Minimum membership period of six months.

  • Net salary must not be below N$1,200.

Regulation & Governance

Public Servants SACCO is regulated under the Co-operatives Act of 1996 by the Registrar of Co-operatives. The Supervisory Committee ensures compliance with regulations, policies and procedures.
